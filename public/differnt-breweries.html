Micro

A Micro Brewery is the name given to most craft breweries which the brewers association defines as “a small and independent brewer.”

Nano

A Nano Brewery is a very small brewery that usually only distributes to local locations.

Regional

A Regional Brewery is a secondary location of another brewery

brewpub

A Brewpub is a brewery that is inside or connected to a bar or restaurant.

Planning

This is a brewery that is currently being built!

Contract

A Contract Brewery is a type of brewery that uses the resources of another brewery.

Closed

This is a brewery that has been closed or is no longer open.
